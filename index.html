About me

Passionate about cinema and graphic art since my childhood, I graduated a bachelor of computer graphics with a specialization in 3D VFX at the HEAJ Namur in Belgium.
Versatility is probably my number one quality. I have interest in nearly every stage of the workflow from the early conception to the fine tuning of the last results. I have various skills from 3D modeling to lighting and color grading as well as animation or rigging.
In the same way, although being primarily oriented towards 3D creation, I also often invest time and skills in 2D creation as bitmap and vector graphic assets are used a lot for those projects. My artistic knowledge is well balanced with technical understanding of the tools I use. I am never afraid to "loose" time and braincells to completely master what I am doing. In this way, I love using node graph architecture softwares or sometimes a little classic programming to create my own procedural tools that I use in my projects.

Since 2023 I also started to work as a freelance audiovisual technician, combining my knowledge of IT, video and sound to find solutions for every situation. I am known for being versatile, reliable and having self control.
